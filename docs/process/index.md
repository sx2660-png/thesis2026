# Process

The process pages record how the project changed over time. They are shorter and more selective than the private working archive: the purpose is to show decisions, not reproduce every note.

- [Updates](updates.md): what was completed, what changed, and what came next.
- [Meeting Notes](meeting-notes.md): key points from enterprise and project meetings.
- [Feedback](feedback.md): feedback that changed the research or design direction.
- [Reflections](reflections.md): short reflections on milestone transitions and design decisions.
