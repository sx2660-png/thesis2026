# Pitch

The pitch materials communicate the project to people who may not already understand electricity trading. They use a concrete opening, explain the decision problem, and show why a human-in-the-loop system is needed.

## Presentations

- [Mid-term Pitch](mid-term-pitch.md): the first public explanation of the problem, context, and proposed system.
- [First-semester Pitch](first-semester-pitch.md): a developing final presentation page for the prototype, evaluation, and reflection.

The presentations are communication artifacts rather than substitutes for the research pages. The project rationale and evidence are documented in [Problem & Context](../problem-and-context.md), [Research](../research.md), and [Prototype](../prototype.md).
